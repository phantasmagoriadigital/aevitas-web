OUR SOLUTIONS

In a garden, only an expert knows the plant for you.

Just like a dedicated financial partner.

We live in an age where a host of investment options are available at our fingertips. However, it takes an expert to study your environment, the market climate and all other conditions to know which are the best options for you. That is where our trusted partners come into the picture. They not only create a special bouquet of investment choices for you by studying your investment objectives, but also help to curate the best options for you.

Financial Solutions

Our wealth managers assist you in overseeing your investment portfolio across asset classes including Mutual Funds, Portfolio Management Service, Alternate Investment Funds, Direct Equity, Structured Products, Global Investments and Fixed Income Instruments.

Estate Planning & Wealth Succession

Inter-generational wealth transfers come with their share of complexities. That is where a trusted wealth partner helps you manage the intricacies of the process, ensuring that your wealth is bequeathed to the intended beneficiaries smoothly and in a tax efficient manner.

Risk Management & Insurance

In order to protect your family, your business and your assets, it is imperative to plan for the unexpected. A robust insurance policy along with an efficient wealth associate is what you need. So you stand to benefit from a thorough assessment of your requirements, options weighed in wholly and a smooth process ensuring policy acquisition and maintenance.

Tax & Legal Support

When it comes to handling taxation and legal formalities, it is best dealt by professionals. So you have in place a systematic tax plan that ensures all entities are tax compliant as well as met with sound legal advice.

Real Estate Advisory

Whether it is residential or commercial properties in Mumbai or Delhi-NCR, our expert team assists you to not just find the best possible option, but also oversee the entire transaction process, guiding you every step of the way.

Start Up & Private Equity Investments

Pre-screened early stage businesses are handpicked after a comprehensive assessment to provide you with opportunities to develop scalable and profitable entities.

Retirement Solutions

For your retirement years to be spent peacefully, it is important to plan ahead with a focus on income generation, keeping capital preservation and growth at the core. Our experienced wealth managers help you achieve these goals seamlessly.

Philanthropy

Our capable wealth managers guide you to make financial allocations for the purpose of philanthropy, supporting projects and endeavours that not just benefit the society at large, but also where donating is the key requirement for investment management for the client.

Global Investing

Our seamless platform works to create an ecosystem for cross-border investments, allowing you to invest in multiple global stock markets affordably and flexibly. Not just that, our experts also cater to NRIs for offshore investments.

Structured Finance Solutions

Corporations with highly specific needs usually resort to structured finance. And in most cases, this involves one or more discretionary transactions, and may often be risky. Our team understands this and therefore engages only with platforms or professionals with credibility, to offer bespoke structured credit products that are largely tailored around untapped cash flows and security.
